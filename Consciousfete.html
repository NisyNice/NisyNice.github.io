Consciousfete (Exploration)
Consciousness is about having knowledge and being aware. This event is tied to an emerging conference that allows individuals to explore space, science, the arts, and all things unknown and forgotten. At night, participants will have access to the space themed night party Consciousfete. Since art is a great representation of consciousness, the expression of human’s creativity and experiences, a portion of each guest’s entry fee will be donated to a non-profit that supports the arts.
