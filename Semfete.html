Semfete - Black Ant - Empowerment
The word Summer stems from the variant of the Proto-Indo-European sem meaning “together/one”. Semfete is an annual Summer party (Summerfete) that represents the idea that the community and the individual are dependent on each other for success. The event themes will represent the many types of communities in our world such as a jungle, ocean, farm and more. Like our neighborhoods, each of these animal communities share many characteristics. Semfete is the opportunity for people who share a neighborhood and often times commonalities, to do community service during the day for their beloved area and at night party for their community. By the end of Semfete, there will be an established togetherness and oneness. A portion of a person’s entry fee to the night party will be donated to a community center or non-profit in support of a specific neighborhood.
